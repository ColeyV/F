About us

Dr. James has been a board-certified Family Medicine physician since 2011. Even before medical school, she dreamed of creating a space where women could feel beautiful, empowered, and inspired. Femme Moderne, "modern woman" in French, is an embodiment of those values. Her goal is to ensure each guest feels truly valued from the inside out, through an elevated aesthetics experience unmatched by anywhere else in Utah.

Hi everyone, I'm Carli! I graduated from Roseman University with a bachelor’s degree in Nursing in 2019 and started my career in a Cardiac step-down ICU before moving into the Emergency Department but, I’ve actually worked in medicine for 10 years! I started my career in Dermatology and have always had a passion for skin care. When I’m not working or learning a new skill, I find enjoyment from backpacking, yoga, reading books and practicing meditation. What I love about aesthetics is the ability to have a part in renewing client’s confidence by helping you become more comfortable in your own skin! The one-to-one time truly allows me to connect with you and help you reach your goals on a personal level. My goal here at Femme Moderne is to help you feel your best so that you can be your best! I’m so excited to meet you!

Leah has been a Registered Nurse for 9 years. She has a passion for health and wellness and enjoys helping others find joy in that as well. She loves spending time adventuring outdoors with her husband and 3 young kids. She is excited to be part of the Femme Moderne team, and is looking forward to helping men and women feel confident with themselves, and enhancing their natural beauty.

Susan Tullis became one of the first Esthetician’s in Utah to start her own Skincare School in 1997 called Skinworks. She enjoyed offering education and training in the esthetics field for over 15 years. Susan also ran a day spa in Sugarhouse called Skinbar . After many years of running businesses and teaching in the Esthetic industry, she decided to change course and focus on medical spa treatments. She is excited to join Femme Moderne Aesthetics where they offer the lastest and most innovative treatments in Medical Esthetics.
